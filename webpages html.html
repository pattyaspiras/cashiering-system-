About Us...


The bakery was established in the year 1995 with its first founder, Sir Manuel M. Lousi. As he passed away his position was then passed to Guill Chong (real name: Irish Micheal Chong.) Miss Chong then handled the whole business until it was passed to one of their family close friends, Sir " Maning".

Sir Manuel Lousi has a passion for cooking and baking and had always dreamed of having a bakery for himself, and that's when the whole bakery plan had gone through â€” turning into a well known and successful bakery business in Cagayan de Oro.

The bakery's first branch was in Colambog, Lapasan. This is where the entire production and selling happened as well as their first office. The business then emerge to different areas and had established other branches inside and outside the city.
The bakery business of Sir Manuel Lousi had been running for almost 27 years, starting from its first establishing year (1995) to the present time.
